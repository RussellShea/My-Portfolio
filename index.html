About the Engineer...

My name is Russell Sheathelm, but it actually wasn't the name assigned to me at birth. It was actually Kim, Min-Woo. I was adopted and hence relinquished from South Korea when I was around 5 months old. I grew up in Rocky Hill, Connecticut where I still reside after 21 years of life. I live with my parents, my sister Tia, and my two dogs Ziggy and Oakley. I am a moody optimist, eager to learn and eager to converse and collaborate. I would describe myself as obsessive and hard-working, caring and reliable, intelligent yet sensible. A fun fact about me is I ran an ebay store selling used vintage electronics such as CRT monitors and early 2000s computers. I am an artist, a guitarist, a gamer, and a homebody extroverted walking contradiction. I am currently a student for Trilogy's Coding BootCamp offered by UCONN. I am available to meet and am looking forward to receiving your call/email.
